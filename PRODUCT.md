# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Flashcards is for anyone who wants to learn and remember anything, especially in short mobile study sessions. Users may be students, professionals, hobbyists, or autodidacts; their shared context is limited time, fragmented attention, and a desire to keep learning moving without setup friction.

## Product Purpose

Flashcards helps people remember more with less friction through a mobile-first spaced-repetition study experience. Success means users can start studying quickly, trust the product to guide review timing, and return regularly because the workflow feels clear, calm, and lightweight.

## Brand Personality

Clear, calm, and reliable. The product should feel focused and supportive rather than playful for its own sake. Copy should be direct, reassuring, and practical.

## Anti-references

Flashcards should not look like an overly gamified or childish education app. Avoid loud rewards, toy-like visuals, busy mascot-driven UI, and dopamine-feed patterns that distract from focused study.

## Design Principles

- Reduce friction before adding features.
- Make the next study action obvious.
- Earn trust through consistency and clarity.
- Respect short attention windows on mobile.
- Support progress without turning learning into a game.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific accessibility requirements are defined yet. Use solid product accessibility defaults: semantic UI, keyboard-accessible controls, sufficient contrast, readable text sizes, clear focus states, and reduced-motion alternatives for non-essential motion.
